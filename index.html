Frequently Asked Questions

Is my collection public?

No. Your vault is secured by Google Authentication and stored privately. Only you can see it.

How do I unlock containers?

Log more items! The Matka unlocks at 5 items, Diary at 15, and Suitcase at 30.

What happens when I click an item?

It triggers 'Inspect Mode', bringing the artifact forward so you can see its exact details and remove it if needed.
