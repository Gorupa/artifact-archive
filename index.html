Explorer's FAQ

Is my collection public?

No. Your sanctuary is secured by Google Authentication. Only you can access your artifacts.

How do I unlock containers?

Preserve more items! The Clay Pot unlocks at 5 items, Journal at 15, and Chest at 30.

How do I view details?

Simply click an item in the grid to pick it up and inspect its history and value.
